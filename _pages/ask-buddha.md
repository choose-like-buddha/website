# Ask Buddha: Mindful Guidance Whenever You Need It

Life rarely gives us clean, simple problems. Most of the time we're stuck somewhere in the middle of a messy conversation, an awkward decision, or a feeling we can't quite name. **Ask Buddha** is the feature in the [Choose Like Buddha app]({{ '/' | relative_url }}) built for exactly those moments — an AI-powered companion that offers calm, thoughtful guidance inspired by centuries of Buddhist teaching.

## What Is Ask Buddha?

Ask Buddha is an on-demand mindfulness assistant. You describe any real-life situation in your own words — a conflict, a decision, a feeling — and Ask Buddha responds with a grounded, compassionate perspective drawn from Buddhist philosophy. It is not a generic chatbot and not a replacement for therapy. It is a quiet, patient voice designed to help you pause, reframe, and respond with more awareness.

## How Ask Buddha Works

1. **Describe the situation.** Type what's happening — the more honest and specific you are, the more useful the response.
2. **Receive a mindful perspective.** Ask Buddha offers a calm reflection, often suggesting where attachment, aversion, or rushed judgment might be shaping your view.
3. **Take what helps, leave what doesn't.** The goal is insight, not instruction. You stay in charge of your own choice.

## When to Use Ask Buddha

- Before sending a message you might regret
- When a conflict with someone you love keeps replaying in your head
- When a decision feels heavy and you can't tell why
- When you're overwhelmed by anxiety, anger, or self-doubt
- When you simply want a mindful second opinion before you act

## Why Ask Buddha Helps You Make Better Decisions

Research on mindfulness consistently shows that even short pauses between impulse and action reduce reactivity and improve decision quality. Ask Buddha builds that pause into your daily life. The act of writing the situation down slows you down. Reading a thoughtful response slows you down further. That small gap — between reaction and response — is where wiser choices live.

Unlike traditional journaling, Ask Buddha responds. Unlike searching online, it doesn't bury you in opinions. It gives you one calm perspective, shaped by one of the oldest wisdom traditions on earth.

## Grounded in Buddhist Wisdom

Ask Buddha draws on core Buddhist concepts — impermanence, compassion, non-attachment, mindful speech, and the middle way — and applies them to modern problems. You don't need to be a Buddhist to benefit. The ideas are practical, universal, and millennia-tested.
